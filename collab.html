Ways to Collaborate

We approach every collaboration with the same standard we hold our essays: depth, honesty, and genuine value to the reader.

Guest Essay

Write a long-form essay for Poetic Codes. We welcome writers with original perspectives and the patience to argue them carefully. Your work reaches a thoughtful, engaged readership.

800+ words · Original only

Podcast / Conversation

Join a recorded conversation on ideas, culture, or technology. We are building a companion audio format for Poetic Codes — in-depth discussions that complement the essays.

30–60 min · Remote or in-person

Educational Partnership

Bring Poetic Codes essays into your classroom, university syllabus, or reading programme. We can provide curated collections, discussion guides, and author Q&As.

Schools · Universities · Libraries

Technical Collaboration

Contribute to the infrastructure of Poetic Codes — from the Firebase backend to the reading experience. We welcome developers who care about the long-form web.

Open source · Firebase · Web

Community Sponsor

Support the publication without advertising. Sponsorship at Poetic Codes means appearing as a credited supporter of independent long-form writing — nothing more, nothing less.

No ads · Transparent · Values-aligned

Open Proposal

Have an idea that doesn't fit any category above? Propose it. Some of the best collaborations are the ones nobody has a name for yet. We are open to anything genuinely interesting.

Any format · Any scope

Send a Proposal

We respond to every serious proposal within 5 business days.

Before You Reach Out

  • Read at least two essays on Poetic Codes so your proposal is grounded in what we actually do.
  • Be specific. "I'd like to write about culture" is not a proposal. "I'd like to write 2,000 words arguing that X because Y" is.
  • We do not accept promotional content, sponsored posts, or link-building requests of any kind.
