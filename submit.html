Submission Guidelines

Essays must be original, unpublished work — not previously posted on any blog, publication, or social media.

Minimum 800 words. Most published essays run 1,500–4,000 words. Take the space your argument needs.

Write for a general but intelligent reader. Define technical terms. Explain your assumptions.

We value intellectual honesty above everything: cite your sources, acknowledge counterarguments.

All submissions are reviewed within 7–14 days. You will receive a response either way.

By submitting, you grant Poetic Codes the right to publish and promote your essay. You retain full authorship and copyright.
