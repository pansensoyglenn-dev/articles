Submission & SEO Guidelines

  • Essays must be original and 500+ words.
  • Blank lines become paragraph breaks in Plain Text mode.
  • HTML mode supports rich formatting — scripts and styles are stripped.
  • The excerpt becomes the meta description — write it carefully for SEO.
  • Tags are comma-separated and help readers discover your essay.
  • URL slug is auto-generated from the title if left blank.
  • Published instantly to homepage, archive, essays list, and article view.
