Impact of Sara Duterte Conviction on Duterte Family Political Dynasty

A conviction of Sara Duterte in her ongoing 2026 impeachment trial would severely undermine the Duterte family's political dynasty, which has dominated Davao City and national politics for decades.[web:27][web:29] Her removal from the vice presidency and lifetime ban from public office would block her frontrunner status for the 2028 presidential race, weakening the clan's national influence.[web:28][web:29]

Dynastic Structure

The Dutertes control key positions: Rodrigo (former president, now ICC-detained), Sara (VP), son Sebastian (Davao mayor), son Paolo (congressman), and grandsons in local roles.[web:32][page:1] A conviction would isolate Sara, Rodrigo's key successor, leaving less viable heirs like the unpopular brothers amid their father's legal woes.[web:29][web:30]

Political Fallout

Marcos Jr. could exploit this to dismantle Duterte strongholds in Mindanao, appointing allies and sidelining family loyalists in upcoming elections.[web:27][web:28] It risks eroding their grassroots base in Davao, where dynasty resilience relies on Sara's appeal, potentially sparking factionalism or reduced turnout.[web:32]
