Strategic Amnesia 101: The Tactic That Ate Itself

Politicians love this gem: Associate turns state's witness on "confidential funds"? Channel your inner goldfish: "Ramil who? The guy who fixed my mic at the rally? Never seen him—poof!"

Wrong. In 2026 Philippines (85M internet users), it's like hiding a carabao in a TikTok filter. Filipino detectives—fueled by lumpia and grudges—hear "I don't know him" and yell "BET!"
