About Poetic Codes

A journal built on the belief that depth still matters — and that the examined life is the only defense against the age of noise.

Why This Website Exists?

Poetic Codes was born from a simple purpose which is to disseminate factual information: the internet is full of information, but starved of understanding. Every platform is optimised for speed, outrage, and engagement — not for the slow, careful work of actually thinking something through.

"I am not here to add to the noise. I am here to give the noise a reason to stop through intensive dissection if facts."

I do publish essays on culture, national affairs, international conditions, and programming — four domains where clear thinking is most urgently needed and most often absent.

The Founder

Founder & Editor-in-Chief

Glenn Junsay Pansensoy

Writer, programmer, and reader of too many books. I started Poetic Codes as a personal archive of ideas that refused to fit into a tweet. It grew into something larger — a community of people who believe that thinking carefully is a form of resistance.

What I Believe

Depth Over Virality

I would rather have one reader finish an essay changed than a million skim it unchanged.

Honesty Over Comfort

I write what I actually think, even when it is inconvenient. Especially when it is inconvenient.

Plurality of Voice

No single perspective can hold the whole truth. I actively seek writers from different backgrounds.

Craft Matters

A well-constructed sentence is a form of respect for the reader. We take the writing seriously.
